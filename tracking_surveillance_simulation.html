What is Location Tracking?

Location tracking collects data about where a device or person is over time. Apps, networks, and services collect location for convenience, but the same data can be used for surveillance or profiling.

  • Device: supplies location (GPS, Wi-Fi, cell towers).
  • Service provider: collects and stores location data.
  • Tracker/Attacker: uses data to build a movement timeline or identify individuals.
