What is Social Engineering?

Social engineering uses manipulation and trust to trick people into revealing secrets or taking actions that compromise security. Phishing is a common form, where attackers send fake messages that look legitimate.

  • Attacker: creates a fake message or website.
  • Victim: receives the message and is tricked into acting.
  • Goal: steal credentials, financial data, or install malware.
